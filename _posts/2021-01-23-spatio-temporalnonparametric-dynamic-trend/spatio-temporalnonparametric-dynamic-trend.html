Spatial Effect

BYM Model (\(u_{i}\)) (\(v_{i}\)) .

(\(u_{i}\))

\[\begin{align*} &u_{i} \vert u_{-i} \thicksim N(m_{i}, s^2_{i}),\\ &m_{i} = \frac{\sum_{j \in \mathcal{N}_{i}} u_{j}}{{\# \mathcal{N}_{i}}} ,\;\; s^2_{i} = \frac{\sigma^2_{u}}{\# \mathcal{N}_{i}}, \end{align*}\]

  • \(\mathcal{N}_{i}\) : \(i\)

  • \(\# \mathcal{N}_{i}\) : \(i\)

  • \(m_{i}\) : \(i\) \(u_{j}\)

  • \(s^2_{i}\) :

      • ,

  • iCAR

(\(v_{i}\))

\[\begin{align*} v_{i} \thicksim N(0, \sigma^2_{v}) \end{align*}\]

  • (Overdispersion)

  • (Parameter) : \(\log{\tau_{u}}\), \(\log{\tau_{v}}\), where \(\tau_{u} = 1/{\sigma^2_{u}}\), \(\tau_{v} = 1/{\sigma^2_{v}}\)

  • \(u_{i}\) \(v_{i}\) , \(\xi_{i}=u_{i}+v_{i}\)

Time Effect

Nonparametric Dynamic Trend (\(\gamma_{t}\)) (\(\phi_{t}\)) .

(\(\gamma_{i}\))

Nonparametric Dynamic Trend (\(\gamma_{t}\)) Random Walk(RW) .

\[\begin{align*} \gamma_{t} \vert \gamma_{t-1} \thicksim N(\gamma_{t-1}, \sigma^2_{\gamma}), \text{RW of order 1},\\ \gamma_{t} \vert \gamma_{t-1}, \gamma_{t-2} \thicksim N(2\gamma_{t-1} + \gamma_{t-2}, \sigma^2_{\gamma}), \text{RW of order 2} \\ \end{align*}\]

  • Dynamically .

(\(\phi_{t}\))

\[\begin{align*} \phi_{t} \thicksim N(0, \sigma^2_{\pi}) \end{align*}\]

  • (Exchangeable) prior Gaussian .
  • (Parameter) : \(\log{\tau_{\gamma}}\), \(\log{\tau_{\phi}}\), where \(\tau_{\gamma} = 1/{\sigma^2_{\gamma}}\), \(\tau_{\phi} = 1/{\sigma^2_{\phi}}\)
